Psalm 67

May God be merciful to us, and bless us
May He grant us the favour of his smile.
Make known thy will, O God, wide as earth; make known among all
nations thy saving power.
Honour to thee, O God,
from the nations, honour from all the nations!
The Gentiles, too, may rejoice and be glad; a whole world
abides thy judgement, and the Gentiles, too, obey on
earth thy sovereignty. Honour to thee, O God, from
the nations, honour from all the nations! The land
has yielded its harvest; such bounty God, our own
God, affords. God grant us ever his blessing, and
may earth, far and wide, do him reverence.
